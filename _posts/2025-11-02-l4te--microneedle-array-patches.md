            <h3 style="margin:0 0 0.5rem;padding-top: 0;">Introduction</h3>
            <p style="margin:0;">Since December 2024, I have been conducting part-time research at the <a href="https://www.l4te.org/">Laboratory of Translational Engineering</a>. My work focuses on the design, fabrication, and testing of <strong>Microneedle Array Patches (MAPs)</strong>. The MAPs team develops <strong>implantable microneedle-based medical devices</strong> as unobtrusive, self-administered long-term contraceptives to <strong>enhance accessibility in developing nations</strong>. My role centers on streamlining manufacturing processes to accelerate testing and iteration of the microneedle patches</p>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div style="flex:0;">
        <h2 style="margin:0 0 0.5rem; text-align: center; padding-top:20px;">Research Overview</h2>
        <p style="margin:0">The design and optimization of fully implantable, polymer-based microneedle array patches for 6-month contraceptive delivery focuses on three core challenges:</p>
        <ul style="margin:10px;">
            <li style="padding:0;"><strong>Skin insertion & base separation</strong>: Identified and characterized biocompatible, rapidly dissolving polymers that enable reliable microneedle insertion and clean detachment from the backing layer upon application. </li>
            <li style="padding:0;"><strong>Controlled long-term release</strong>: Performed bench-top release kinetics and compaction studies to achieve zero-order drug release over 180+ days, increasing drug loading efficiency by >50%.</li>
            <li style="padding:0;"><strong>Scalable manufacturing</strong>: Developed precision silicone molds and vacuum compression molding protocols, reducing per-patch production time and boosting component yield.</li>
        </ul>
        <p style="margin:0">Additionally, the microneedle array patches are fabricated at the <strong>micron scale</strong>, demanding extreme precision in both engineering and quality control to achieve viable yields. Through systematic root-cause analysis, we identified and eliminated previously catastrophic micron-level defects that had plagued production. This effort honed an <strong>exceptional eye for detail</strong>, <strong>a disciplined approach to scrutinizing every process parameter</strong>, and <strong>deep expertise in microfabrication troubleshooting and defect mitigation</strong>.
        </p>
